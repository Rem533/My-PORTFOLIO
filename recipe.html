Fluffy Omelette

Prep Time

5 mins

Cook Time

10 mins

Servings

1 person

Difficulty

Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp / 15g butter
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 tbsp cream (or 1 ½ tbsp milk)
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1/3 cup grated cheese
  • 1 tbsp / 15g butter or olive oil
  • 150 g / 5 oz mushrooms
  • 1 small garlic clove, minced
  • Pinch of fresh thyme leaves and finely chopped parsley (optional)

Procedure

  1. Mushroom Filling: Melt butter in a medium or small non-stick skillet over high heat.
  2. Add mushrooms and cook until starting to go golden on the edges – about 1 ½ minutes.
  3. Add garlic, salt and pepper, cook until golden – about 1 ½ minutes. Remove from skillet.
  4. For the omelette, whisk eggs, cream, salt and pepper.
  5. Cool skillet and lower heat to medium.
  6. Add butter, then when it foams, add egg. Immediately give it a few stirs, then when the base just starts to set, stop stirring.
  7. Sprinkle cheese in a line down the middle, top with mushrooms.
  8. Using a spatula, fold one side over the fillings, then the other side.
  9. Slide onto a plate. Garnish with herbs if desired and enjoy!
