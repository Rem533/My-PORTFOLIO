Beef Pilau

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1/4 kg beef, prepared boiled
  • 2 tbs Whole Pilau Masala
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 grated ginger and 1 grated garlic
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 3 tbs cooking oil
  • 3 mugs boiling water

Instructions:

  • 1. Prepare the ingredients together to avoid wasting time.
  • 2. Fry onion till golden brown.
  • 3. Add garlic and ginger and fry. But don't let it burn.
  • 4. Add the whole Pilau masala and cook till the aroma fills the house.
  • 5. Add your pre-boiled beef and fry till golden brown.
  • 6. Add rice and salt and mix well. Then add boiling water and let it cook till the water has reduced.
  • 7. Reduce heat, then turn the Pilau and cover tightly. Let it cook perfectly.
  • 8. Serve with Kachumbari.
  • 9. Tip: If you want a more colored Pilau or a browner Pilau, use double the quantity of whole Pilau masala.
